## Captions

#### Gimbal

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gimbal
Gimbal with 3 axes of rotation. When two gimbals rotate around the same axis, the system loses one degree of freedom.

#### Gimbal lock

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gimbal_lock
Gimbal-locked airplane. When the pitch (green) and yaw (magenta) gimbals become aligned, changes to roll (blue) and yaw apply the same rotation to the airplane.
